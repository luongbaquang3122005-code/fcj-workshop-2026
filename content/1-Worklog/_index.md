**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Getting familiar with AWS and basic AWS services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [IAM & Security](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Learning about EC2](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Learning about VPC](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Learning about S3 + RDS + CloudWatch](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Learning about CloudFront](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Learning about Security WAF + GuardDuty](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Learning about Database PostgreSQL](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Learning about Node.js(BE)+React(FE)](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Develop and deploy full project](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Optimize system architecture and thoroughly test system functionalities](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Finalize and present the project](1.12-week12/)
